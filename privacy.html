Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters to us

Effective Date: January 1, 2025

1. Information We Collect

FreePromptHub is designed to respect your privacy. We collect minimal information:

2. How We Use Information

The limited information we collect is used to:

  • Improve our website and user experience

3. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Analytics: To understand site usage patterns

4. Cookies

We use cookies to:

  • Remember your preferences

You can choose to disable cookies through your browser settings, though this may affect your experience on our site.

5. Affiliate Links

Our site contains affiliate links. When you click these links and make a purchase, we may receive a commission. These transactions are processed entirely on third-party sites, and we do not collect or store any of your payment information. Please review the privacy policies of any third-party sites you visit.

6. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction of information. However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure.

7. Children's Privacy

Our site is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Effective Date" above.
