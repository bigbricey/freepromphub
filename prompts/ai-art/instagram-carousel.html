üí° Pro Tips for Viral Carousels

  • The 3-2-1 Rule: 3 seconds to hook, 2 key points per slide, 1 clear CTA
  • Swipe Indicators: Always show there's more content (arrows, "1/10" counter)
  • Value Stacking: Each slide should deliver value, even standalone
  • Save-Worthy: Make slide 9 a summary people want to save for reference
  • Color Psychology: Use consistent colors - changes confuse the flow
  • Text Hierarchy: Headlines 2x bigger than body text for scannability
