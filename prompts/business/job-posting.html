Job Posting Writer

Attract the right candidates and repel the wrong ones with job postings that actually work.

Example Use Case

Position: Customer Service Rep for e-commerce company

Honest Hook: "Love helping angry customers become fans? We get 50+ complaints daily and need someone who sees problems as puzzles, not stress."

Pro Tips

  • Use the actual job title, not creative versions
  • Put salary range in first paragraph - saves everyone time
  • Describe a typical day, not vague responsibilities
  • Be honest about the hard parts - builds trust
  • Include 1-2 screening questions in application
  • Post on Tuesday for maximum visibility
