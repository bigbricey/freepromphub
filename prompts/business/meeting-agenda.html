Meeting Agenda Creator

Stop wasting everyone's time with pointless meetings. Create agendas that drive decisions and get stuff done.

Example Use Case

Meeting: Q1 Marketing Strategy Session

Agenda Item: "Social Media Budget (10 min) - DECIDE: Allocate $5K between Facebook, Instagram, LinkedIn. NEED: Last quarter's ROI data. OUTCOME: Final budget split approved."

Pro Tips

  • Send agenda 24 hours before meeting minimum
  • Start with quick wins to build momentum
  • Assign a note-taker who isn't leading
  • Set phone/laptop rules at the start
  • End every topic with "who does what by when"
  • Cancel if you can solve via email instead
