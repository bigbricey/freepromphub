Product Launch Plan

Turn your product launch from crickets to cash with a step-by-step launch plan that builds hype and drives first-week sales.

Example Use Case

Product: Online course about Instagram growth

Launch Strategy Generated: 7-day open cart with declining bonuses each day. Day 1: Course + 1-on-1 call ($497). Day 7: Course only ($297). Result: 60% of sales on Day 1 and Day 7.

Pro Tips

  • Start building hype 30-60 days before launch
  • Create scarcity with limited quantities or time
  • Get 10-20 beta users for testimonials pre-launch
  • Launch on Tuesday-Thursday for best engagement
  • Send 3 emails on launch day (morning, afternoon, evening)
  • Have customer support ready - expect questions
