Customer Complaint Response

Turn angry customers into loyal fans with professional, empathetic responses that actually solve their problems and protect your reputation.

Example Use Case

Situation: Customer posted 1-star review saying their order never arrived

Response Generated: "Hi Sarah, I'm personally looking into this right now - this isn't the experience we want for any customer. I've found your order (#12345) and see it shows delivered, but clearly something went wrong. I'm sending a replacement today with expedited shipping at no charge. You'll receive tracking within 2 hours. I'm also including a 20% discount code for the inconvenience. Please email me directly at owner@business.com if you need anything else."

Pro Tips

  • Respond within 1 hour for public complaints (social media/reviews)
  • Move the conversation private quickly but acknowledge publicly first
  • Never argue or get defensive, even if customer is wrong
  • Overdeliver on the solution when possible - surprises create fans
  • Follow up after resolution to ensure satisfaction
