Pricing Strategy Calculator

Stop guessing what to charge. Get data-driven pricing recommendations that maximize profit while keeping customers happy.

Example Use Case

Situation: Web designer charging $500 per website, struggling to get clients

AI Recommendation: Switch to 3-tier pricing: Starter ($997), Professional ($2,497), Premium ($4,997). Position against DIY options, not other designers. Include payment plans. Result: Higher perceived value, 3x average sale price.

Pro Tips

  • Price based on value delivered, not time spent
  • Always test prices higher than you're comfortable with
  • Offer 3 options - most pick the middle one
  • End prices in 7 or 9 for psychological impact
  • Bundle services to increase average order value
  • Raise prices 10-20% yearly for existing customers
