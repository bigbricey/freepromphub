Business Contract Generator

Create simple contracts that protect you and get you paid. No lawyer speak, just clear agreements that work.

Example Use Case

Contract For: Website design project - $5,000

Key Protection Added: "Client provides all content within 7 days of each milestone or timeline extends accordingly. Designer retains rights to display work in portfolio."

Pro Tips

  • Always get contracts signed before starting work
  • Be specific about what's included AND what's not
  • Include a kill fee if client cancels mid-project
  • Define "revision" clearly to avoid scope creep
  • Use online signing tools for legal timestamp
  • Keep contracts under 3 pages when possible
