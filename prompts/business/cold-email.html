Cold Email Templates

Get responses from cold emails instead of crickets. Write outreach that converts strangers into conversations.

Example Use Case

Selling: SEO services to local businesses

Subject Line: "Quick question about [Company Name]'s Google visibility"

Opening: "Hi [Name], I noticed [Company] ranks #8 for '[keyword]' but your competitor [Competitor] owns the #1 spot. That's probably costing you 5-10 customers per week. Takes 5 minutes to show you how to flip that - interested?"

Pro Tips

  • Research for 2 minutes, write for 1 minute
  • Reference something specific about them in first line
  • Lead with their problem, not your solution
  • Keep subject lines under 5 words
  • Send Tuesday-Thursday, 10am or 2pm their time
  • Follow up 4x - 80% of responses come from follow-ups
