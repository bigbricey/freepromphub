Employee Onboarding Plan

Get new employees productive in days, not months. Create onboarding that reduces turnover and builds loyalty from day one.

Example Use Case

New Hire: Junior Developer at startup

Day 1 Win: "Push first code commit by lunch - pre-selected simple bug fix that builds confidence and gets them in the workflow immediately."

Pro Tips

  • Send welcome email 3 days before start date
  • Assign a buddy who isn't their manager
  • Give them a real project by day 3
  • Schedule CEO/founder meet within first week
  • Check in daily for first week, then weekly
  • Celebrate first win publicly to team
