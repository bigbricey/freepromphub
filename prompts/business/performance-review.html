Performance Review Writer

Write performance reviews that improve performance. Give feedback that's honest, helpful, and legally safe.

Example Use Case

Situation: Good performer with communication issues

Feedback Example: "Your technical work on the API project was excellent - zero bugs in production. To reach the next level, focus on communicating progress proactively. For example, the June deadline surprise could have been avoided with earlier updates."

Pro Tips

  • Document throughout the year, not just at review time
  • Use specific examples, not general impressions
  • Balance every criticism with recognition
  • Focus on behaviors, not personality
  • Make goals measurable with deadlines
  • Have employee write self-review first
