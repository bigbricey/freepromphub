Sales Script Generator

Create sales scripts that convert browsers into buyers. Handle every objection and close with confidence.

Example Use Case

Selling: Website design services to local businesses

Opening Hook Generated: "Hi John, I noticed your restaurant's website is sending customers to your competitors. Takes 30 seconds to show you - can I share my screen real quick?"

Objection Handler: "I understand price is a concern. What's it costing you right now to lose 10-15 customers a week to restaurants with better websites?"

Pro Tips

  • Talk 30%, listen 70% - ask questions constantly
  • Never argue with objections - acknowledge and redirect
  • Use customer's exact words when summarizing
  • Create urgency with limited-time bonuses, not pressure
  • Always get a specific next step, never "I'll follow up"
  • Record your calls to hear what really works
