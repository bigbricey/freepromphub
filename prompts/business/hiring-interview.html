Hiring Interview Questions

Create interview questions that actually reveal if someone can do the job and fit your culture. Stop hiring mistakes before they cost you thousands.

Example Use Case

Position: Sales Representative for SaaS company

Key Question Generated: "Tell me about a time you lost a big sale. Walk me through what happened and what you learned." (Good answer: Takes responsibility, specific learnings. Bad answer: Blames others, no growth shown)

Pro Tips

  • Ask same questions to all candidates for fair comparison
  • Use "Tell me about a time when..." for real examples
  • Listen for specific details - vague answers = red flag
  • Ask about failures and conflicts - everyone has them
  • Have them do actual work samples, not just talk
  • Trust your gut - if something feels off, it probably is
