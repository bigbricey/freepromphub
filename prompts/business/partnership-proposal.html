Partnership Proposal Generator

Structure partnership deals that benefit both sides. Stop leaving money on the table with weak proposals.

Example Use Case

Partnership: Local gym partnering with meal prep service

Proposal Hook: "Your members ask about nutrition 10x more than equipment. We can solve that problem and add $2,000/month to your revenue with zero effort from your staff."

Pro Tips

  • Lead with their benefit, not your need
  • Show specific numbers and projections
  • Start with a small test before going all-in
  • Make it easy to say yes with clear terms
  • Address their concerns before they voice them
  • Follow up 3 times before giving up
