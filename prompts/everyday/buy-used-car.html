Buy Used Car Smart

Don't get ripped off buying a used car. Get a complete inspection checklist and negotiation strategy.

Example Use Case

Situation: Looking at 2018 Honda Civic with 45,000 miles

Key Question to Ask: "Why are you selling after only 2 years? Can I see service records for the 30,000 mile maintenance?"

Negotiation Point: "KBB shows $18,500 for this condition. You're asking $20,000. I can do $18,000 cash today."

Pro Tips

  • Always get pre-purchase inspection ($100-200)
  • Test drive for at least 30 minutes
  • Check tires - uneven wear = problems
  • Never buy without seeing title in person
  • Best deals are October-December
  • Walk away if seller won't let mechanic inspect
