Car Maintenance Planner

Save thousands on car repairs by knowing what really needs fixing and when. Stop shops from selling you stuff you don't need.

Example Use Case

Car: 2015 Toyota Camry with 75,000 miles

Priority Fix: "Brake pads at 3mm - replace within 2 weeks ($300). Transmission flush can wait until 100k miles despite shop recommendation."

Pro Tips

  • Oil changes: Follow manual, not 3,000 mile myth
  • Tire rotation every 5,000-7,500 miles
  • Check fluids monthly (takes 5 minutes)
  • Replace air filter yourself ($20 vs $50)
  • Keep all service records for resale value
  • Find independent shop - 30-50% cheaper than dealer
