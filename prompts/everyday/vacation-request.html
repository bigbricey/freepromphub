Vacation Request That Gets Approved

Get your time off approved even when it's inconvenient. Frame your request so bosses have to say yes.

Example Use Case

Request: Two weeks off during busy season

Winning Line: "I've arranged coverage with Sarah for daily tasks and will complete the Q3 report before I leave. I'll also be available by email for any emergencies."

Pro Tips

  • Request time off 2-3 months in advance
  • Offer to work extra before/after to compensate
  • Present solutions, not problems
  • Document approval in writing always
  • Book flights AFTER approval
  • Be flexible on exact dates if possible
