Get Cheaper Insurance

Stop overpaying for insurance. Get scripts to negotiate lower rates and find discounts you're missing.

Example Use Case

Current: Paying $200/month for auto insurance

Script That Worked: "I've been a customer for 5 years with no claims. Progressive quoted me $140. I'd prefer to stay with you - can you match that or get close?"

Result: Got reduced to $150/month - saved $600/year

Pro Tips

  • Shop insurance every 6-12 months
  • Increase deductible to $1000 if you have emergency fund
  • Drop comprehensive on cars worth under $4,000
  • Bundle auto + renters/home for 15-25% off
  • Ask about: good student, military, professional discounts
  • Pay 6 months upfront for additional 5-10% off
