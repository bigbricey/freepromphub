Neighbor Complaint Handler

Resolve neighbor issues without making enemies. Get what you need while keeping things civil.

Example Use Case

Problem: Upstairs neighbor's loud music at 2 AM

First Approach: "Hey! I'm in 2B below you. Your music sounds great but it's coming through my ceiling at night. Would 10 PM work as a quiet time? I work early and would really appreciate it."

Pro Tips

  • Try face-to-face before written complaints
  • Bring cookies/beer for first conversation
  • Document dates/times of issues
  • Record noise/take photos if needed
  • Know quiet hours laws in your city
  • Consider mediation before legal action
