Doctor Visit Prep Guide

Stop wasting doctor visits. Get your health issues actually addressed with the right preparation and questions.

Example Use Case

Issue: Chronic fatigue for 6 months

Key Question: "I've tracked my fatigue - it's worst at 2pm daily and impacts my work. What tests can rule out thyroid, anemia, and sleep disorders? What else should we check?"

Pro Tips

  • Write symptoms down - don't rely on memory
  • Bring someone if discussing serious diagnosis
  • Ask for visit summary in writing
  • Don't accept "it's just stress" without tests
  • Get copies of all test results
  • Book first appointment of day for more time
