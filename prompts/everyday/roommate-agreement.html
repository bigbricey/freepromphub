Roommate Agreement Creator

Avoid roommate drama with clear agreements upfront. Cover everything from dishes to guests before it becomes a fight.

Example Use Case

Common Issue: Dirty dishes piling up in sink

Agreement Rule: "Dishes must be washed within 24 hours or moved to your room. After 48 hours, dirty dishes go in a box outside owner's door."

Pro Tips

  • Discuss everything BEFORE move-in day
  • Put agreements in writing, even with friends
  • Set up Venmo/Zelle for bills on day 1
  • Create a group chat for house communication
  • Schedule monthly check-ins to adjust rules
  • Be specific about "clean" - everyone's different
