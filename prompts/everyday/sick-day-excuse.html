Professional Sick Day Message

Take a sick day without damaging your reputation. Send messages that sound professional and believable.

Example Use Case

Situation: Food poisoning, important meeting today

Message: "Hi [Boss], I'm unable to come in today due to illness. I've asked Tom to cover the 2pm client meeting (he has all materials). I'll handle the proposal remotely if I'm feeling better later. Planning to return tomorrow."

Pro Tips

  • Keep it simple - don't over-explain
  • Notify ASAP - before work hours if possible
  • Offer solutions for urgent items
  • Never lie about family emergencies
  • Save sick days for when truly needed
  • Mental health days are valid sick days
