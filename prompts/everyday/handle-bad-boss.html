Handle Bad Boss

Deal with a toxic boss while protecting your sanity and career. Document everything and plan your exit strategy.

Example Use Case

Situation: Boss yells in front of others

Response Script: "I want to address your concerns. Can we discuss this in private?" Then document: "On [date] at [time], [boss] raised voice regarding [topic] in front of [witnesses]."

Pro Tips

  • Document everything - dates, times, witnesses
  • BCC personal email on important things
  • Never badmouth boss to coworkers
  • Build relationships with boss's peers
  • Have 6 months expenses saved before confronting
  • Your mental health is worth more than any job
