Get Security Deposit Back

Stop landlords from stealing your deposit. Create documentation that forces them to return your money.

Example Use Case

Situation: Landlord claims carpet needs replacement after 2 years

Response: "Per [State] law, carpet has 5-year depreciation. After 2 years, carpet is worth 60% of original value. Normal wear is expected and not chargeable to tenant."

Pro Tips

  • Take 100+ photos on move-out day
  • Video walkthrough with timestamp
  • Email everything - create paper trail
  • Know your state's deadline (usually 30 days)
  • Send forwarding address via certified mail
  • Small claims court usually costs $30-50
