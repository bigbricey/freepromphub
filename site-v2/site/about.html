About FreePromptHub

FreePromptHub is a community-driven library of production-ready prompts across models and domains. It’s lightweight, open, and designed for speed.

  • Open: use, remix, and contribute freely.
  • Practical: real-world prompts curated by practitioners.
  • Accessible: fast, responsive, and keyboard-friendly.

Built here as a static site for reliability and portability—no build system required.
